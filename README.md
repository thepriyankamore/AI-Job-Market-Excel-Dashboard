# AI Impact on Jobs 2030 Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the potential impact of Artificial Intelligence on jobs across various industries using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into future job demand, salary trends, AI replacement risk, and job satisfaction.

## Dataset

Dataset: AI_Impact_on_Jobs_2030

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers
* KPI Cards
* Dashboard Design

## Key KPIs

* Future Demand Score: 60%
* Average Salary: $126,642
* AI Replacement Risk: 50%
* Job Satisfaction Score: 3.03

## Key Insights

* Finance industry shows the highest average salary.
* Technology industry faces higher AI replacement risk.
* Healthcare demonstrates relatively lower automation risk.
* Future job demand remains strong across several industries.
* Moderate job satisfaction indicates opportunities for workplace improvement.

## Dashboard Features

* Future demand analysis by industry
* AI replacement risk by job title
* AI replacement risk by industry
* Average salary analysis
* Interactive slicers and filters

## Skills Demonstrated

* Data Cleaning
* Data Analysis
